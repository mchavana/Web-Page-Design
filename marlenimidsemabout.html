About us

Luna's was started by Mario and Theresa Camoochio in 1904. It began as just a local family bakery, serving traditional Italian favorites. Over the years, it has expanded to serve a variety of deserts, including tradional favorites and modern fusion pieces. Luna's also does custom desert making and will create the perfect signature desert for your event. Luna's is the perfect mix of classic style with a modern twist!
