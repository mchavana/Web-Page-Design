Welcome to Luna's!

Luna's is a bakery in the heart of Westfield. It is the one stop shop for all your desert needs. With a variety of deserts ranging from old school classics like lobster tails and eclairs, to new fusion pieces like Taro cupcakes and the rainbow canoli. We are able to create custom cakes and pastries for any occasion. Our chefs specialize in the artistry of piping, giving all of our deserts a classic and elegant look. Please look to Luna's for all your desert needs!

We are open everyday from 7am to 9pm! Stop in to get a pastry and say hello!

Located at 138 North Ave. next to the Kings Super Center!
